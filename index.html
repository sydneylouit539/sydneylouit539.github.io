Sydney Louit

Hello and welcome to my site! I'm a Ph.D. candidate in statistics at the University of Connecticut, where I teach and research. I am 24 years old and come from Weston, CT.

I am currently doing my Ph.D. at the University of Connecticut (Storrs), under the supervision of Prof. Jun Yan, with the degree expected Summer 2025. During the school year, I also teach undergraduate statistics classes. Courses in which I have been the primary instructor can be found below.

Outside of academics, I am an avid traveler and hiker. As of early 2025, I have visited 11 countries, 41 states, and 436 counties, and hiked 15 state highpoints and 1 national highpoint.

Some other projects are available on my GitHub, including March Madness models, a chessbot, and an R package containing functions and utilities for my thesis.
